Wahana Ciptasinatria Strengthens Position as Indonesia’s Leading End-to-End IT Solutions Provider

Written by Bunga Abdul
· 04 Sept 2025 · 4 min read
Copy Link
Share on Facebook
Share on X
Share on LinkedIn

Background

Adira Finance is one of the leading financial service providers in Indonesia, offering financing solutions for automotive, multipurpose loans, and other consumer finance products. With a rapidly expanding customer base and increasing reliance on digital platforms, Adira Finance required a reliable, secure, and scalable IT infrastructure to support day-to-day operations and future growth. The company’s IT team faced the challenge of modernizing its infrastructure while ensuring compliance with strict industry regulations and maintaining uninterrupted customer service.

Challenge

As digital transformation accelerated in the financial sector, Adira Finance faced several challenges:

  • Growing demand for reliable connectivity: Increasing numbers of branches and customers required a robust backbone to ensure consistent performance.
  • Business continuity risks: Existing Disaster Recovery Center (DRC) solutions were not optimized, raising concerns about downtime during potential disruptions.
  • Security threats: Handling sensitive financial data demanded enhanced security systems to prevent breaches and protect customer trust.
  • Communication inefficiencies: Legacy voice and communication systems were limiting collaboration and responsiveness across the organization.

Adira Finance needed a trusted partner to provide an integrated, future-ready solution that could overcome these obstacles while preparing the organization for long-term digital growth.

Solution

Wahana Ciptasinatria (WCS) partnered with Adira Finance to deliver comprehensive end-to-end IT solutions. The project included:

  • MPLS Core Switch & Enterprise Backbone: Designed to strengthen connectivity across branches and core systems, ensuring high-speed, reliable performance.
  • Data Center (DC) and Disaster Recovery Center (DRC) Infrastructure: Implementation and optimization of systems that guaranteed data redundancy, minimized downtime, and ensured regulatory compliance. Voice System Solutions: Upgrading legacy systems with advanced voice solutions to improve internal communication, customer service, and contact center efficiency.
  • Enterprise Network Security: Deployment of security measures, including firewall systems, intrusion prevention, and data encryption, to protect against evolving cyber threats and safeguard sensitive financial data.

Throughout the project, WCS provided strategic consulting, seamless system integration, and ongoing support to ensure a smooth transition without disrupting Adira Finance’s day-to-day operations.

Innovative Technology Partnerships

As a vendor-neutral consulting firm, WCS collaborates with globally recognized IT vendors to provide the most suitable and cutting-edge solutions for its clients. This allows WCS to deliver enterprise-grade services such as cloud ERP, Microsoft Dynamics AX, advanced network security, disaster recovery, and digital vendor management systems, empowering businesses to improve efficiency, reduce risks, and prepare for future growth.

Commitment to Excellence

“Our mission has always been to deliver solutions that not only meet immediate business needs but also prepare our clients for long-term success in a rapidly changing digital landscape,” said the management team at WCS. “By combining global best practices with local market expertise, we ensure our clients gain maximum value from their IT investments.”
