Industries We Serve

With over two decades of experience and more than 200 successful projects, Wahana Ciptasinatria (WCS) has become a trusted partner across multiple industries. Our end-to-end IT solutions are designed to meet the unique challenges of each sector, driving innovation, efficiency, and growth.

Financial Services

We support leading banks and financial institutions in strengthening their IT infrastructure, securing networks, and modernizing core systems.

Clients include: BRI, BTN, BNI, Mandiri, Adira Finance, Bukopin.

Projects delivered: WAN backbone, data center upgrades, enterprise switching, VPN solutions, and network security systems.

Telecommunication

Our expertise enables telecom providers to expand their connectivity, manage large-scale networks, and deliver reliable digital services.

Clients include: Cable & Wireless, Satkomindo, Telkom, Bakrie Telecom, Beacon Telecommunication.

Projects delivered: Gigabit Ethernet, multimedia communication, wireless LAN, backbone systems, and network management platforms.

Oil & Gas

We help energy companies secure and optimize their operations with advanced IT infrastructure and communication solutions.

Clients include: Pertamina (Balongan, Plaju, Cilacap), Talisman Energy, Conoco Philips.

Projects delivered: Network backbone, PABX systems, gigabit switching, and next-generation connectivity solutions.

Public Services

From aviation to utilities, we support public service providers in modernizing operations, enhancing security, and ensuring reliable IT systems.

Clients include: Angkasa Pura II, Jasa Angkasa Semesta, Humpuss Holding, PDAM Surabaya, Power Gen, Meratus Group.

Projects delivered: LAN core switching, ERP implementation, CCTV networks, and enterprise backbone systems.
