Empowering Businesses with
Innovative IT Solutions Since 1995

Wahana Ciptasinatria (WCS) is an end-to-end IT solution provider in Indonesia. Established in 1995 under the Cormic Technology Group, we deliver business solutions, IT infrastructure, ERP, cloud services, and digital transformation consulting to enterprises nationwide.With offices in Jakarta, Surabaya, and Bandung, and a team of 220+ technology specialists, WCS has successfully executed 200+ projects across industries including finance, telecom, oil & gas, and public services.

What We Do

Our Core Solutions:

  • Infrastructure Solutions: Data centers, storage, and end-user computing.
  • Network Solutions: Integration, security, and voice solutions.
  • Applications Solutions: ERP, cloud, and e-business platforms.
  • Services Solutions: Maintenance, helpdesk, and network support.
  • Human Resources Solutions: HCM, payroll, and recruitment systems.
  • Vendor Management Solutions: E-registration, e-catalogue, invoicing, and tracking.

Trusted by Leading Organizations

WCS has supported top enterprises and institutions in Indonesia, including Bank Rakyat Indonesia, Bank Mandiri, Telkom Indonesia, Pertamina, and Angkasa Pura. Our experience spans finance, telecommunications, oil & gas, and public services, giving us the depth to serve clients of all sizes and sectors.

Our Commitment

At WCS, we don’t just provide technology—we deliver solutions that drive business outcomes. Our mission is to help organizations accelerate digital transformation, reduce costs, and improve operational efficiency while ensuring security and compliance.
